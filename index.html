Designer turned Software Developer.

I began my career in the United States Marine Corps as a Combat Videographer. My service afforded me numerous opportunities to not only film our nation's warriors at work, but to also design graphic artwork for commands aboard Marine Corps Base Hawaii. Following an honorable service, I focused my studies on the application of design fundamentals, social/behavioral sciences, and basic programing skills in a digital media context at Savannah College of Art and Design. Being awarded a Bachelor of Arts in Interactive Design and Game Development has not been the final step in my journey.

My collegiate studies not only set in motion a desire for user-centered design, but also an unquenchable thirst for learning more about programming languages as applied to Web Development. Que my studies at Codeup. This opportunity to study under Codeup's vibrant Computer Software Professionals has been instrumental in directing my career path toward being a Front-end Software Developer. The Codeup curriculum has prepared me to become a Full-Stack Web Developer.
